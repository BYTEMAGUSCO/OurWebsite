Who, What, Where, When, and Why is ByteMagus

BYTEMAGUS CO. is a tech company founded by Julette Anthony C. Peque and Romnick R. Canonigo, focused on advancing technological modernization in the Philippines. Originally a passion project, the company specializes in web systems development, computer system support and management, and network support. The name "BYTEMAGUS" represents their innovative and playful approach, symbolizing a "computer wizard" dedicated to technological progress.

Our Vision

To become a leading force in the technological modernization of the Philippines by 2030, known for our expertise, innovation, and commitment to excellence. We envision a future where BYTEMAGUS CO. plays a pivotal role in shaping a digitally advanced nation, bridging the gap between technology and its practical applications for every Filipino.

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge technological solutions that empower businesses and individuals in the Philippines. We aim to provide exceptional services in web systems development, computer support, and network management, fostering innovation and driving digital transformation across the country.
